Frequently Asked Questions

1. What types of businesses do you work with?

We work with a wide range of businesses, from small startups to large enterprises. Our services are tailored to meet the unique needs of different industries, including e-commerce, healthcare, education, and more. Whether you need a simple website or a complex web application, we can help.

2. How long does it take to build a website?

The timeline for building a website depends on the complexity of the project. For smaller websites (5-10 pages), it typically takes 2-4 weeks. Larger or more complex projects, such as e-commerce platforms or custom web applications, may take anywhere from 6-12 weeks. We’ll provide a detailed timeline after understanding your requirements.

3. Will my website be mobile-friendly?

Yes, every website we build is fully responsive and designed to perform seamlessly on all devices, including smartphones, tablets, and desktops. We follow a mobile-first design approach to ensure your site delivers an optimal user experience across platforms.

4. Do you offer ongoing support and maintenance after the website is live?

Absolutely! We provide ongoing support and maintenance services to ensure your website remains up-to-date, secure, and performing at its best. Our Enterprise Package includes 1-year support, and we offer tailored maintenance plans to suit your needs.

5. Can you help improve my website’s SEO?

Yes, SEO optimization is a key part of our services. We integrate SEO best practices into every website we build, ensuring it’s search engine-friendly from the start. Our Growth and Enterprise packages offer advanced SEO services to help improve your search engine rankings and drive more traffic to your site.
